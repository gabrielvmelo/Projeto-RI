Series Info

Four estranged best friends are reunited one year after the queen bee of the group, Alison, goes missing. When they begin to receive disturbing messages from someone named "A", how far will they go to bury their secrets? ABC Family's Pretty Little Liars is a relationship drama filled with fun, mystery, intrigue, and secrets. Don't miss even one episode of this exciting new series!
Creator: I. Marlene King
Starring: Lucy Hale, Troian Bellisario, Ashley Benson
